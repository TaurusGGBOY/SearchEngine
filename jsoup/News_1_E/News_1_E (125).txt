BEIJING April Xinhua A State Council executive meeting concluded Wednesday has called for benefiting more people when they seek medical care The conference called for further promoting a pilot medicine procurement program short supply medicine monitoring and coping measures as well as medical assistance The pilot medicine procurement program was tested in major cities to lower drug prices and reduce the burden on patients A total of drugs were selected and their prices were cut down by percent on average with the biggest drop rate over percent The medicine procurement program should be further improved while quality supervision and supply guarantee of selected drugs should be strengthened so as to lessen the price and benefit the people
