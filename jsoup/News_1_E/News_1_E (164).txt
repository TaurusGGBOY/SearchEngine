KUALA LUMPUR April Xinhua Chinese shuttlers cruised into Malaysia Open semifinals on Saturday securing champions already in three categories Lin Dan and Chen Long who shared the past three Olympic gold medals are set to meet in the men s final Lin beat world No seed and fellow teammate Shi Yuqi and after an hour long match Chen made to the final after a turn around against Indonesian Jonatan Christie China s mixed double pair world No Wang Yilyu and Huang Dongping squashed their Malaysian opponents Lai Pei Jing and Tan Kian Meng in a brief encounter lasting minutes They will face world No and fellow compatriots Zheng Siwei and Huang Yaqiong in the final The women s doubles champion will be decided between Chinese pairs Du Yue Li Yinhui and Chen Qingchen Jia Yifan while in the men s doubles final China s Li Junhui Liu Yuchen will play against Takeshi Kamura and Keigo Sonoda of Japan In women s singles No seed Chen Yufei was quickly knocked out by world No Tai Tzu Ying of Chinese Taipei Tai s opponent in the final is Akane Yamaguchi of Japan
