QINGDAO April Xinhua Naval delegations from over countries Saturday began to arrive in the port city of Qingdao east China s Shandong Province to join multinational naval events commemorating the th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People s Liberation Army PLA Navy The events will be held between April and and a naval parade will be held in Qingdao and nearby sea areas and airspace on April A naval delegation from Fiji was the first foreign naval delegation to arrive at an airport in Qingdao at a m Saturday followed by naval delegations from Argentina and Peru Over countries including Russia and Thailand will send vessels to join the events The events will serve as a platform for the participants to boost understanding and share experience on maritime security and cooperation
