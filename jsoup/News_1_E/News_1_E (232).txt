ACCRA March Xinhua Ghana s Black Stars beat the Harambee Stars of Kenya in the final round of the Africa Cup of Nations AFCON qualifiers Saturday night Debutant Caleb Ekuban snatched the match winner for Ghana in the rd minute at the game played at the Accra sports stadium to give the West Africans the needed win against the impressive Kenyan side Both teams played for pride as they had already secured tickets for the AFCON aa However with the win the Black Stars top Group F with nine points followed by Kenya on seven points The AFCON with its new team format will be held from June to July and the Black Stars will be hoping to end the country s year trophy drought
