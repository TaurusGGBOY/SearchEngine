BEIJING April Xinhua China saw a total of million railway trips during the three day tomb sweeping holiday up percent year on year according to the national railway operator The China Railway Corporation said it added trains during the holiday to handle the increase and ensure safe and orderly trips April saw a total of million railway trips setting a one day record for the tomb sweeping holiday Tomb sweeping Day also known as Qingming Festival is an important occasion for Chinese to honor their ancestors Many also spent the three day holiday on leisure travel According to figures from the Ministry of Culture and Tourism China saw million domestic trips during the holiday up percent from last year s holiday
