RIO DE JANEIRO April Xinhua Two time Brazilian Serie A champions Botafogo sacked head coach Ze Ricardo on Friday following a poor run of results The decision came less than a day after Botafogo lost to Juventude a result that eliminated the Rio de Janeiro side from the Copa do Brasil The club would like to thank the manager for his work dedication and professionalism Botafogo said in a brief statement Ricardo led Botafogo to wins draws and losses after taking charge last August Among the candidates to replace the year old are former Cruzeiro boss Marcelo Oliveira and Jair Ventura the son of Brazil World Cup winner Jairzinho Ventura has already been Botafogo s coach once before having held the position from August to December The Brazilian Serie A season begins on April
