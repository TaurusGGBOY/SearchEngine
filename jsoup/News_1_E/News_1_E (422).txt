LONDON March Xinhua Raheem Sterling scored a hat trick as England thrashed Czech Republic in their opening match of the Euorpean championship qualifying campaign at Wembley here on Friday England dominated the match and pulled ahead in the th minute when Sterling positioned perfectly at the far post to tap in from a low cross from former Manchester City teammate Jadon Sancho The City forward then earned a penalty just before the interval and captain Harry Kane made no mistake from the spot Sterling made it in the nd minute when the ball deflected off a Czech player and fell into his path then he held off his marker Ondrej Celustka turning smartly and bending into the left corner He completed the hat trick only six minutes later when he cut into the middle and shot from yards with an effort which deflects off Celustka and beat the goalkeeper He became the first England player to score a hat trick at Wembley since Jermain Defoe in Tomas Kalas own goal in the th minute made it and also ended their misarable night
