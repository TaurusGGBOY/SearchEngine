BEIJING April Xinhua Top Chinese swimmers will take part in the FINA Champions Swim Series a leg competition held in meter pool between April and May According to the FINA website Sun Yang Xu Jiayu and Fu Yuanhui are among the swimmers invited by FINA to take part in the event Sun Yang is the absolute protagonist and will shine in men s m freestyle and men s m freestyle in each of the three legs with six races in total Former world champions Xu Jiayu and Fu Yuanhu will also compete in the backstroke races men s m backstroke men s m backstroke women s m backstroke and women s m backstroke respectively The FINA Champions Swim Series is a brand new event that invites the top swimmers around the world and distributes almost US million in prize money It features a unique competition format where only finals will be held with four swimmers per final Each of the legs will have individual races The first stop of the competition will take place in Guangzhou China from April to Hungary s capital Budapest which hosted last year s FINA world championships will stage the second leg competitions on May Indianapolis of the United States will round off the series from May to June Other
