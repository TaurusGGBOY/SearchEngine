ANKARA April Xinhua Turkish Foreign Minister Mevlut Cavusoglu said on Friday that Turkey understands NATO s concerns over its procurement of the Russian S air defense missiles We are aware of NATO s concerns It is not right to say that Turkey is not considering them Cavusoglu said at a press conference with his Romanian and Polish counterparts in Ankara adding that Turkey needs air defense systems urgently His remarks came after the U S government claimed that Turkey s adoption of Russian missiles could cause security risk on F jets and NATO defense system and threatened to suspend the sale of F jets to Turkey unless it scraps the S deal Cavusoglu said that Turkey offered the United States to form a working group to determine whether the S missiles pose a threat to the F fighter jets There are S s in Syria and F jets have flown over them repeatedly If those claims are true the missile systems would have already gathered enough information about the aircraft Cavusoglu said Last week Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan reiterated that Turkey s procurement of the Russian S air defense systems is a national security deal criticizing the United States for halting the F deliveries
