HANOI March Xinhua Vietnam will be one of the first countries in the world to roll out G the latest generation of mobile communications its minister of information and communications said here on Friday Minister of Information and Communications Nguyen Manh Hung made the remarks at the ASEAN Conference on G in Hanoi regarding the role of G as a pillar of digital economy due to its greater bandwidth higher speeds and lower latency Vietnam News Agency reported The Vietnamese Information and Communications has licensed local carriers to test G services in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City he said adding that the country is also focusing on developing G based technologies and equipment Vietnam is willing to cooperate with other members of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations ASEAN to establish joint ventures and factories to make information technology equipment the minister said By the end of Vietnam with a population of nearly million had roughly million fixed broadband Internet subscribers and million fixed and mobile phone subscribers including million mobile phone ones according to the country s General Statistics Office The conference was jointly held by the ministry the Vietnam Internet Association and the International Data Group
