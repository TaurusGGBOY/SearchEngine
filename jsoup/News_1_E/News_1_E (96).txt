BEIJING April Xinhua China saw million domestic tourist trips during the three day tomb sweeping holiday up percent from last year s holiday according to the Ministry of Culture and Tourism The tourism revenue reached billion yuan about billion U S dollars during the holiday which lasts from Friday to Sunday up percent according to the ministry While tourists still favor sites of natural scenes an increasing number of people chose to visit history museums martyrs memorial halls and memorial sites of revolution during the holiday the ministry said in a report adding that the online ticket sales at such spots across the country saw a year on year increase of percent The Tomb sweeping Day or Qingming Festival is an important occasion for Chinese people to honor their ancestors Many also spent the three day holiday on leisure travel
