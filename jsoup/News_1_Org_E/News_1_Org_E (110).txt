WASHINGTON, March 22 (Xinhua) -- Chinese number one Wang Qiang was hardly tested before beating Britain's Johanna Konta 6-4, 6-0 to advance into third round at the Miami Open on Friday. Wang, seeded 18th in the tournament, needed just one hour and 24 minutes to secure the victory. Earlier this month, Wang made the round of 16 at the BNP Paribas Tennis Open. Enditem
